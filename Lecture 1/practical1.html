6. Run dIVW using mr.divw

divw.res<-with(dat,mr.divw(beta.exposure, beta.outcome, se.exposure, se.outcome))

#dIVW estimate and SE

7. Interpretation of the MR estimate

LDL-C is continuous and was standardized prior to GWAS. CAD is binary and logistic regression was used in GWAS. Thus, the MR estimate indicates a 57% (exp(0.45)-1) increase in the odds of CAD per 1-SD increase in LDL-C level.
